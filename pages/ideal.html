Idealization

  • We examine a two-body system, comprising Earth and the Sun, under the following assumptions:
  • We exclude gravitational effects of all other celestial bodies.
  • We assume the orbit of the planet to be elliptical and Sun at one of its foci.
  • We consider both the Sun and the Earth to be point masses in the system.
